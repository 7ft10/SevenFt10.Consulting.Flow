# Priority

o   Straightforward

o   Frequent

o   Document needed

o   No authorisation is needed

## Normal

o   Important

o   Full review

o   Requires CAB authorisation

## Major

o   High risk

o   Detailed report

o   Requires CAB & management authorisation

## Emergency

o   Urgent

o   Resolves incident

o   High risk of failure

o   Flexible pool of approvers
